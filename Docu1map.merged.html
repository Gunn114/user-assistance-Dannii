How to start the Calculator App

Here you will find information on how to start the Calculator app on your mobile device.

To use the calculator app, you need to unlock your phone first

You can use a calculator app on your smartphone. You will find it useful, when you need to quickly calculate some numbers.

  1. Navigate to the Calculator application icon
  2. Tap the icon

The application will launch. You are now ready to calculate with it

For example, if you have, purchased a book for £5 and you have given £10, how much change should you expect? Answer is 10-5=5 in change.
